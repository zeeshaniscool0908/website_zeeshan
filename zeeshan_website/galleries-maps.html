The Holy Empire of Akhribah

The H.E.A. is my most involved creative project — a fictional Islamic empire I've been constructing for years. It has its own constructed language (Akhribz), complete cartography, a detailed system of government, an Islamic-inspired legal framework (sharia-based jurisprudence), national flags, cultural norms, and a rich history. It's a place where my love of linguistics, history, Islamic studies, and cartography converge into something that's entirely my own.
