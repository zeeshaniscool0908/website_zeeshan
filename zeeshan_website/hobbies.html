Interests

My Hobbies

Outside class, this is where my time goes.

🗺️

World-Building: H.E.A.

The Holy Empire of Akhribah is my most involved creative project — a fictional Islamic empire with its own language (Akhribz), maps, government, and jurisprudence. I've been building it for years and it continues to grow.

🖨️

3D Printing

I print on a Bambu Lab P1S. I design custom models, iterate on builds, and constantly push what the machine can do. It's where engineering meets artistry for me.

🖊️

Calligraphy

Arabic and English calligraphy. The patience, precision, and beauty of the craft resonates deeply with my aesthetic values and my faith.

🎮

Minecraft

I build elaborate structures and worlds in Minecraft — often inspired by H.E.A. It's creative play and architectural experimentation in one.

📜

History

Empires, civilizations, and the forces that shaped humanity. I read widely across Islamic history, South Asian history, and classical antiquity.

💪

Fitness & Nutrition

I take fitness seriously as a form of discipline and self-investment. Training the body is as important as training the mind. I track nutrition and train consistently.
