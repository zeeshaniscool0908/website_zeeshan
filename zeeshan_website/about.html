Biography

In My Own Words

I am Syed Zeeshan Ahmad, raised in Loudoun County, ethnically Pakistani, religiously Muslim, and politically in the center. I am a creative and hard-working individual. I have a loving family, and all of them are special to me.

My academic interests are Pharmacology, History, and Linguistics. These three fields represent something profound to me — understanding how things heal, how civilizations rise and fall, and how language shapes human thought.

I am currently a student at Penn State University Park, Class of 2030, pursuing a path that I hope will one day contribute to research on rare genetic disorders and the medicines that can treat them. My motivation is deeply personal.

Beyond academics, I spend my time building — whether that's models on my Bambu Lab P1S, calligraphy on canvas, or an entire fictional empire called the Holy Empire of Akhribah with its own language, maps, and Islamic-inspired governance. I believe creativity and scholarship are the same impulse.
